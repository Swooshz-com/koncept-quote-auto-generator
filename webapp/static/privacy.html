Privacy Notice

Swooshz Quote Generator Privacy Notice

This notice explains how the app handles quotation data, uploaded booth images, AI analysis, logs, and support diagnostics.

Repository Baseline

Production behavior must conform to docs/privacy-pdpa-gdpr-baseline.md. This page is the user-facing notice; the repo baseline is the engineering source of truth for future production work.

Data We Process

  • Uploaded booth render/reference images used for AI quote-basis analysis.
  • Customer, project, quotation, company header, payment term, note, and signature fields entered in the app.
  • Pricing references, imported catalog rows, aliases, tax settings, and profile presets managed by admins.
  • Generated quote basis rows, pricing review rows, quotation spreadsheets, and related output files.
  • Usage, status, and error diagnostics needed to operate, secure, and support the app.

AI Provider Processing

When you run analysis, uploaded images and relevant quote context may be sent to the configured AI provider. The provider, model, analysis mode, image count, status, and usage/cost details should be logged per user in production when available.

Local Development Behavior

In the local app, generated files are written under local repo output folders and support logs are written under the root _logs folder. Pricing references saved through the settings flow are stored as repo pricing-reference packs. Local role permissions may be simulated with environment settings.

Security And Logs

User-facing errors should stay short: failed, please try again, and contact support if the issue keeps happening. Detailed diagnostics belong in server-side logs with a support-safe error reference. Logs must not expose secrets, API keys, Authorization headers, cookies, raw images, stack traces, or unnecessary customer text.

Your Rights And Requests

In production, users should be able to request access, correction, deletion, export, restriction/objection, or withdrawal of consent where applicable under PDPA, GDPR, or other relevant privacy laws. Support contact and retention periods must be finalized before external launch.

Production Readiness

Before external launch, the live deployment needs authenticated user/account partitioning, role-based access, retention/deletion jobs, AI provider/sub-processor review, cross-border transfer review, support workflows, and legal review of this notice.

Swooshz Quote Generator Privacy Notice

This notice explains how personal data may be collected, used, disclosed, stored, and protected when you use the Swooshz Quote Generator.

Personal Data We May Collect

We may collect information you provide when preparing quotations, including names, job titles, company names, addresses, project details, uploaded reference files, quotation content, and related communications.

How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data to prepare quotations, manage customer and project records, provide requested services, respond to enquiries, improve our workflow, maintain security, and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

Disclosure Of Personal Data

We may disclose personal data to service providers, contractors, professional advisers, authorities, or other parties where necessary for quotation preparation, service delivery, business operations, legal compliance, or protection of rights.

Cross-Border Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside your country, we will take reasonable steps to ensure that the receiving party provides a standard of protection consistent with applicable PDPA, GDPR, or other relevant privacy laws.

Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this notice, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal, or similar risks.

Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, or withdrawal of consent for personal data we hold about you.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the organisation responsible for the quotation or service relationship.
